Sri Lanka Government Budget Analysis

Strategic Data Story · Public Policy

The Challenge

National budgets are typically published as dense, inaccessible documents. This makes it difficult for the public and even policymakers to understand key fiscal priorities. The challenge was to simplify this complexity into a clear and engaging format without losing important details.

My Role

As the lead Information Designer, I was responsible for the entire visual strategy. My work involved analysing the raw budget data, designing a cohesive visual language, and developing a series of straightforward infographics and charts. I acted as the bridge between the raw data and public understanding.

The Outcome

The resulting visualisations were widely shared on social media and used in policy briefs by researchers and journalists. The project made complex fiscal information accessible, fostering a more informed public debate on national spending priorities and showing how design can make crucial government data available to everyone.
